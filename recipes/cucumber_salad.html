Cucumber Salad

Description

An easily made cucumber salad with dill!

Ingredients

  • 4 thinly sliced cucumbers
  • One red onion sliced to half circles
  • One quarter of a cup of chopped dill leaves
  • 3 spoons of olive oil
  • 5 spoons of lemon juice
  • salt and pepper as much as you like

Steps

  1. Stir all the ingredients in a bowl and enjoy!
