Fried Egg

Description

The most basic recipe of fried eggs!

Ingredients

  • One or two eggs
  • Just a bit of cooking oil

Steps

  1. Spill a bit of cooking oil in a pan
  2. Heat the pan to at least 200 degress celsius
  3. Crack the eggs straight on the pre-heated pan. Wait for 2 minutes
  4. It's recommended to flip the eggs and cook it for one minute more, but it's not mandatory
